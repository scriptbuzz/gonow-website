## Production Checklist

### 1. Data Seeding (Critical)
Before recording, ensure your "Demo Account" looks lived-in:
*   [ ] **Map**: Have at least 10-15 active routes visible.
*   [ ] **Drivers**: Set 3-4 drivers to "Active/Green" status.
*   [ ] **Analytics**: Ensure the graphs show a positive trend (green lines going up).
*   [ ] **Dates**: Make sure data dates match the recording date (or conceal dates).

### 2. Recording Settings
*   **Resolution**: Record in **1080p (1920x1080)** minimum. 4K is better if possible.
*   **Scale**: If recording on a high-res monitor, set browser zoom to **125% or 150%**. This makes text readable on mobile screens without zooming in post-production.
*   **Mouse**: Use a tool (like Screen Studio or a mouse highlighter) to make your cursor easier to follow.

### 3. Editing Tips
*   **Speed Ramps**: Accelerate the "Loading/Processing" time of the "Optimize Routes" step. It should look instantaneous in the video (even if it takes 2s in real life).
*   **Crops**: Don't show the browser URL bar or bookmarks bar. Crop strictly to the app interface.
