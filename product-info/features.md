# Features Overview

GoNow is a comprehensive logistics ecosystem designed to synchronize every part of your delivery operation. Below is an overview of our core modules. For technical specifications and deep dives, refer to the individual module pages.

## 1. [Smart Route Planning](./features-planning.md)
*AI-powered route orchestration and assignment.*
- Smart Route IDs (#YYMMDD.n)
- AI-Assisted task descriptions
- Drag-and-drop schedule management
- Real-time team assignment

## 2. [Reliable Verification (PoD)](./features-verification.md)
*Building trust through indisputable photo evidence.*
- Advanced camera (zoom/flash/gallery)
- Store name, GPS, and Time watermarking
- Hive-style secure storage organization
- 3-Photo limit per stop for compliance

## 3. [Warehouse & Inventory](./features-warehouse.md)
*Precision picking and loading before the trip.*
- Consolidated picking manifests
- Per-truck loading plans
- Multi-page professional PDF slips
- Real-time inventory status monitoring

## 4. [Analytics & Performance](./features-analytics.md)
*Data-driven insights into your fleet's efficiency.*
- Completion rate & performance metrics
- Historical delivery logs
- High-performance on-demand data fetching
- Professional PDF and CSV exports

## 5. [Enterprise Management](./features-management.md)
*Control and security for scale.*
- Strict RBAC (Role-Based Access Control)
- Detailed login and audit logs
- Connectivity & System health heartbeat
- Disaster recovery & data snapshots
