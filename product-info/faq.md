# Frequently Asked Questions

### Is GoNow mobile-friendly?
Absolutely. GoNow is designed with a **Mobile-First** approach. It works seamlessly on all iOS and Android browsers, and can be easily wrapped as a native app for enhanced performance.

### How secure is the delivery data?
We use **Firebase Enterprise-grade security**. Your data is protected by strict role-based access rules, ensuring that only authorized personnel can view or edit sensitive information.

### Does it support multiple languages?
Yes. GoNow features full, native support for both **English and Arabic**, including specialized Right-to-Left (RTL) layout optimization.

### Can I export data for accounting?
Yes. You can export all your delivery logs, customer data, and performance metrics into **CSV or JSON** formats, making it easy to integrate with your existing accounting or ERP software.

### What is "Verified Photo Proof"?
It's a feature that requires drivers to take photos of the delivery. The app automatically adds a watermark with the **Store Name, GPS location, and Timestamp**, preventing fraudulent delivery claims.

### Can I manage my warehouse with this?
Yes! GoNow includes specialized **Warehouse Manifests** and **Loading Plans** to help your team pick items and load trucks accurately before they hit the road.
