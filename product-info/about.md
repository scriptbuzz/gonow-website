# About GoNow

GoNow was born from a simple observation: **Logistics is the heartbeat of modern commerce, but managing it shouldn't be a headache.**

Our mission is to provide small and medium-sized delivery teams with the same high-tech tools used by global giants. We believe that efficiency, transparency, and data-driven decisions should be accessible to every warehouse manager and driver.

Founded in **2026**, GoNow has quickly evolved from a simple route tracker into a comprehensive delivery ecosystem. By combining advanced web technologies like **React and Firebase** with real-world logistics insights, we've created a platform that is both powerful and incredibly easy to use.

Whether you're delivering groceries, furniture, or medical supplies, GoNow is here to ensure your journey is optimized, your team is empowered, and your customers are satisfied.
