# Feature Detail: Analytics & Performance

## Overview
GoNow transforms raw delivery logs into actionable insights. Our analytics engine provides high-level visibility for executives while maintaining granular detail for fleet managers.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Delivery Status Distribution
- **Visual Dashboards**: Interactive Pie Charts and Bar Graphs showing the ratio of "Delivered" vs "Missed" vs "Pending" tasks.
- **On-Demand Fetching**: The reporting module uses a decentralized data-fetching pattern, ensuring reports load in milliseconds even with thousands of records.

### 2. Employee Performance Metrics
- **Completion Rates**: Track the percentage of successful deliveries per driver.
- **Volume Tracking**: See who is handling the most volume and who is most efficient on the road.
- **Comparison Views**: Evaluate team performance across custom date ranges (Today, Last 7 Days, This Month, or Custom).

### 3. Deep-Dive Visit History
- **Customer Trajectory**: View a chronological log of every delivery ever made to a specific customer.
- **"Never Visited" Filter**: Instantly identify customers who have been ignored or missed to prevent churn.
- **Sorting & Grouping**: Organize history by Region, City, or Employee for regional performance analysis.

### 4. Enterprise Data Export
- **Professional PDFs**: Download complete performance summaries with charts and summary tables for management meetings.
- **CSV Data Dumps**: Export raw transaction data for deep-dive analysis in Excel or integration with external ERP/BI tools.
- **Admin JSON Backups**: Full snapshot capability for disaster recovery and off-site data archival.
