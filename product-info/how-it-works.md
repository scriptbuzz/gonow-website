# How GoNow Works

Experience a seamless workflow from the warehouse to the customer's doorstep.

### Step 1: Design & Optimize
Upload your customer database and build routes using our intuitive interface. Use **Quick Route Creation** to group multiple stops into a single, efficient delivery run.

### Step 2: Dispatch & Assign
Assign routes to your team members with a single click. Drivers receive instant updates on their mobile devices, complete with address details and Google Maps integration.

### Step 3: Execute & Verify
Drivers follow the optimized route, using the built-in **Verified Camera** to capture watermarked photo proofs at every stop. Status updates are synced in real-time back to the manager dashboard.

### Step 4: Analyze & Report
Review daily performance through automated reports. Download **PDF Loading Slips** for the warehouse and **Analytics Reports** for management meetings.
