# Feature Detail: Warehouse & Inventory

## Overview
A delivery run is only as good as the picking and loading process. GoNow bridges the information gap between the warehouse team and the logistics fleet, ensuring that "what leaves the warehouse is what arrives at the store."

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Consolidated Picking Manifests
- **Aggregate Demand**: The system scans all routes for a selected date and generates a single, consolidated list of every product unit required.
- **Unit Precision**: View picking requirements by Piece, Box, Case, or Bag, according to your product metadata.
- **Header KPIs**: Real-time totals for "Total Units to Pick" and "Active Route Count" displayed at the top of the manifest.

### 2. Strategic Loading Plans
- **Per-Truck Instructions**: View a breakdown of which specific products belong to which Route/Driver.
- **Stop-by-Stop Breakdown**: (Coming soon) View the sequence of delivery to load the truck "Last-In, First-Out" (LIFO) for maximum driver efficiency.
- **Real-time Sync**: If a manager adds a route stop in the office, the warehouse screen updates instantly to reflect the updated picking volume.

### 3. Professional PDF Slips
- **One-Click Generation**: Generate clean, print-ready PDF loading slips.
- **Multi-Page Support**: Large routes are automatically paginated with clean headers and footers.
- **Timestamped Documentation**: Every slip includes a "Generated At" stamp for paper-trail auditing.

### 4. Live Inventory Monitoring
- **Stock Thresholds**: Toggle "In Stock" status for any product to immediately notify managers during the route planning phase.
- **Product Metadata**: Track prices, SKUs, and categories to ensure the right items are being loaded for the right customers.
- **Search & Sort**: High-speed filtering of the inventory list by quantity, price, or name.
