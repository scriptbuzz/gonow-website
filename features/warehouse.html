Warehouse & Inventory

A delivery run is only as good as the picking and loading process. GoNow bridges the information gap between the warehouse team and the logistics fleet, ensuring that "what leaves the warehouse is what arrives at the store."

Consolidated Picking Manifests

  • Aggregate Demand: Scans all routes and generates a single, consolidated list of products required.
  • Unit Precision: View picking requirements by Piece, Box, Case, or Bag.
  • Header KPIs: Real-time totals for "Total Units to Pick" and "Active Route Count".

Strategic Loading Plans

  • Per-Truck Instructions: Breakdown of specific products belonging to each Driver.
  • LIFO Loading: Stop-by-stop breakdown to load trucks for maximum efficiency.
  • Real-time Sync: Instant updates to reflect office-side route modifications.

Professional PDF Slips

  • One-Click Generation: Print-ready loading slips.
  • Multi-Page Support: Automated pagination for large routes.
  • Timestamped Documentation: "Generated At" stamp for paper-trail auditing.

Live Inventory Monitoring

  • Stock Thresholds: Toggle "In Stock" status for immediate planning feedback.
  • Product Metadata: Track SKUs, prices, and categories.
